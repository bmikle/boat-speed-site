Boat Speed in Knots

A dead-simple speedometer for the water.

What it does

Your current speed, in knots, shown big enough to read at a glance from across the cockpit. That's it — no clutter, no menus, no setup. Open it and go.

  • Large, glanceable knots readout
  • Lock-screen Live Activity — see your speed without unlocking your iPhone
  • A standalone Apple Watch app, so your speed is right there on your wrist
  • Day and night themes for bright decks and dark passages
  • Trip max and average speed

Save your battery

GPS is power-hungry. Tap Stop when you don't need a live reading — it shuts location down and keeps your battery for when you really need it. Closing other navigation and chartplotter apps while you're at it helps even more.

Stay safe out there

Boat Speed shows GPS speed over ground — a handy reference, not a substitute for proper seamanship. Keep a lookout, watch the weather, and never rely on a single device for navigation. Fair winds.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Boat Speed does not track you, and it collects no information about you whatsoever.

No data collection

The app has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking or SDKs. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal data — there is no data to collect.

Location

Boat Speed uses your device's GPS for one purpose only: to calculate and display your current speed on your screen, in real time. Your location is used entirely on your device. It is never recorded, never stored, and never transmitted off your device — the app does not connect to any server, and we operate no servers that could receive it.

The Apple Watch app & Live Activity

The Apple Watch app works the same way and stores nothing. The Lock-Screen Live Activity shows your current speed on your own device only.

Children

Because the app collects no data at all, it collects no data from children. It is safe for users of any age.
